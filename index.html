Cross-Country Race Timing App

About Our App

The Cross-Country Race Timing App revolutionizes race management by providing instant race results and digitizing the timing process. Designed for race organizers and participants, our app streamlines timing and management, making cross-country races more efficient and accessible.

With seamless integration with Google Drive, the app allows organizers to store and share race data securely, ensuring real-time access to results for participants and spectators.

Features

  • Instant race result updates
  • Digital timing for accurate and efficient race management
  • Secure storage of race data via Google Drive integration
  • User-friendly interface for organizers and participants
